# Privacy Policy for EMI Studio (EMICalculator)

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EMI Studio (package name `com.aryan_raval.EMICalculator`) handles information when you use the app. EMI Studio provides loan EMI calculators, loan comparison tools, investment calculators, currency conversion tools, crypto conversion tools, and related finance-planning utilities.

## Information We Collect

EMI Studio does not require users to create an account and does not ask for sensitive banking credentials, card numbers, passwords, government IDs, contacts, photos, camera access, microphone access, or precise location.

The app may process the following information:

- Calculator inputs entered by you, such as loan amount, interest rate, tenure, dates, fees, investment amount, currency amount, and selected currencies. These values are used only to calculate and show results inside the app.
- App preferences stored on your device, such as selected language and onboarding status.
- Approximate technical and device-related information collected by third-party services used in the app, including Google Mobile Ads and Firebase Remote Config.
- Network request information sent to exchange-rate and crypto-price providers when you use live currency, historical rate, or crypto conversion features.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide EMI, loan, investment, affordability, savings, currency, and crypto calculations.
- Save basic app preferences on your device.
- Show advertisements and sponsored content when enabled.
- Fetch live exchange rates, historical exchange rates, crypto prices, and remote app configuration.
- Improve app reliability, performance, and user experience.
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and invalid ad activity.

## Local Storage

EMI Studio uses device storage to remember basic preferences, including selected language and whether onboarding has already been completed. These preferences stay on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the app.

## Ads and Third-Party Services

EMI Studio uses third-party services that may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

- Google Mobile Ads / AdMob: Used to display banner, native, interstitial, rewarded, and app-open ads. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share information such as IP address, advertising ID or other device identifiers, app interactions, and diagnostics for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.
- Firebase Remote Config: Used to remotely configure app behavior, messages, ad settings, and app update settings. Firebase may process technical information such as app identifiers, package name, country code, language code, time zone, platform version, OS version, SDK version, and Firebase installation identifiers.
- ExchangeRate-API (`open.er-api.com`): Used to fetch live currency exchange rates.
- Frankfurter API (`api.frankfurter.app`): Used to fetch historical currency exchange-rate chart data.
- Binance API (`api.binance.com`): Used to fetch live crypto price data.

Sponsored links may open inside an in-app WebView. Websites opened through sponsored content or external links may collect information under their own privacy policies.

Third-party privacy references:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google AdMob: https://admob.google.com/home/privacy/
- Firebase Privacy and Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- Binance Privacy Notice: https://www.binance.com/en/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share calculator inputs with our own server.

Data may be collected or shared by third-party SDKs and services listed above for purposes such as advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, service delivery, and app configuration.

## Permissions

The app uses internet access to load ads, remote configuration, currency rates, crypto prices, and sponsored pages. Some Android builds may include storage-related permissions generated by the build framework, but the app does not use those permissions to collect personal files, photos, videos, or documents.

## Children's Privacy

EMI Studio is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Data Security

We use reputable third-party services that transmit data over secure connections where supported. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention and Deletion

EMI Studio does not maintain user accounts and does not store calculator history on our own servers. App preferences stored locally can be deleted by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies.

## Financial Disclaimer

EMI Studio provides calculation estimates for informational and planning purposes only. Results may vary based on lender terms, fees, taxes, market rates, exchange-rate provider data, and timing. The app does not provide financial, investment, legal, or tax advice.
